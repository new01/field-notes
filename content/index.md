## What To Check

- Hover internal links to test popover previews.
- Open the right sidebar on any note to inspect the table of contents, backlinks, and graph.
- Click tags like `demo` and `journal` to verify tag pages.
- Use the explorer to confirm folder pages are populated.

## Demo Focus

- The markdown demo page includes callouts, quotes, highlights, footnotes, tasks, and tables.
- The technical demo page covers code blocks, LaTeX, Mermaid, and inline HTML.
- The knowledge graph demo keeps backlinks, aliases, and cross-note linking active without relying on embeds.
